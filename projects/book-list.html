Winning Books

I have been trying for many years to read the Modern Library top 100 novels of the 20th century. Sites with the list can be cumbersome to navigate on the fly. Now I have this handy site ready to go in the browser in my phone whenever I am at a library or bookstore. You can mark as read and sort by any column. It's exactly what I needed and it had the added bonus of letting me try out development with Svelte for the first time. This was coded by hand before I started using agentic coding for personal projects. Previously, I used a command line interface tool to do the same thing, but this site enables me to take my list with me in my phone.
